the practice

slow skin care.
honest results.

we don't sell quick fixes. each appointment is a full hour at minimum, set aside for one person at a time. the work is unhurried, the conversation is private, and the products are chosen for what your skin actually needs — not what we have in stock.

01

read

every first visit begins with a careful skin reading — under good light, without a clock running. we ask about your routine, your history, and what brought you in.

02

treat

a treatment chosen for today's skin, not last month's plan. cleanse, exfoliation, modalities matched to your needs, and a soft lymphatic finish.

03

tend

you leave with a written plan and a small kit of samples. nothing is upsold. if you don't need a serum, we won't sell you a serum.

the studio

a single room. one face at a time.

mei skin is a one-practitioner boutique built around the belief that good skin care is unhurried. set inside we do beauty studio on west broadway, there's a single treatment room, a single appointment slot per hour, and a single person caring for your skin from start to finish.

we work with plant-based and pharmaceutical-grade actives — no marketing buzzwords, no surprise add-ons, no upselling at the front desk. the prices on the menu are the prices you pay.

services

the menu.

signature facial

90 min

a full skin reading, double cleanse, custom exfoliation, masque, lymphatic massage, and a tailored finish. the visit to start with.

deep cleanse

60 min

for congestion, breakouts, and texture. enzyme exfoliation, extractions handled with care, blue-light, and a calming masque.

microcurrent lift

75 min

low-level electrical current that re-educates facial muscles. immediate lift in the cheekbone and jaw, cumulative over a series.

chemical peel

45 min

medical-grade aha/bha or jessner peels for tone, texture, and pigmentation. depth chosen with you after a thorough consult.

led light therapy

30 min

red light for repair and inflammation, blue light for breakouts, near-infrared for deeper recovery. ideal as a single-session add-on.

microneedling

75 min

collagen induction therapy for fine lines, acne scarring, and overall skin density. numbing, a precise pen depth, and gentle aftercare.

full menu including brow shaping, lash tinting, and body services available on request. members receive priority booking and pricing.
